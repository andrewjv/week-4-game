Instructions

  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game

  • There are four crystals. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

  • You win the game by matching total points to the random number. You lose if your number goes above the random number

  • The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.

  • Each time the game starts, the game will change the value of the crystals.
